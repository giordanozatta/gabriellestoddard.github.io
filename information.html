Gabrielle Stoddard
Overview, Projects, Information

Gabrielle Stoddard has a lens based practices commonly filtered through print media. Practised within commercial and conceptual photography and printmaking. Editiotal, Fashion photography and Product photography has been on going in Stoddard's Practise.

Conceptually Informed by interested of personal experiences some in relation to past experiences with agoraphobia and outer-body- experiences which evoke interest in indeterminate and intangible experience. A focus on a critical reflection on photographs traditions and how to decontextualise photographs in many ways. The notion of a non image is a common query in Stoddard's works.
